EPF & Compliance

How to correct EPF account name mismatch?

We file Form 15G/H with Aadhaar/PAN proof. Typically resolves in 5-7 working days.

EPF Timing & Process FAQs

When should I delink a wrong UAN from my mobile?

Immediately if you're not receiving OTPs or see incorrect details.
Process: Go to your member portal ,you could see delink option click on it and follow the next steps as suggested by portal. NOTE: D-Link will only work If you are not genuinely worked in the mentioned company or no contributions made by the employer.

Best time to submit EPF Form 13 for transfer?

Within 6 months of job change.
Why? Avoids interest loss and simplifies tracking.

When to claim EDLI insurance after death?

Within 3 years of member's death.
Fast-track: Weekdays 10AM-3PM have quicker approvals.
